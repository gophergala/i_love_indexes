About

The idea

The idea behind “I Love Indexes” is pretty simple: developers often setup "Index of" pages to host files and share them easily. However, over the years, we tend to accumulate things inside of them. In order to take back control of these pages, a search engine is a must-have!

How it works

Once the URL of an "Index of" is added, our crawler written in Go will start recursively crawl the url and extract information from the results. Those information are then indexed by Elasticsearch. We use Elasticsearch as the search engine backend, but also as a simple database for our application.


This project was made in 48h during the Gopher Gala, a hackathon organized from January 23 to 25.
