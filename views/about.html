About

The idea

The idea behinde I Love Indexes is pretty simple; as developers we often setup "Index of" pages to host files, but over the years we tend to accumulate things inside of it. In order to take back control of those once easily accessible and friendly directories a search engine is needed!

How it works

Once an url of "Index of" is added, our crawler written in Go will start recursively crawl the url and extract information from the results. Those information are then indexed by Elasticsearch. We use Elasticsearch as the search engine but also the database of our system.

I Love Indexes let you easily search files within "Index of" web sites.
